I've been playing Pokemon TCG Pocket for some months now. Here is a small showcase of some of my favorite full-art cards from the game, as well as an explanation on why I chose each one.

Gastrodon

Set:
A2 | Space-Time Smackdown

This artwork, by Takashi Shiraishi, reminds me of the Van Gogh painting "The Starry Night" because all the elements seem to be blended together, but somehow still distinguishable. A friend of mine who's a graphic designer told me the specific art style this was based on, but I forgot what it was, haha.

Glaceon EX

Set:
A2a | Triumphant Light

I love how this artwork reminds me of a watercolor painting. The almost pastel-ish hue of colors I believe accentuates Glaceon's elegance really well. I've been wanting to get this card for months now, but I've had no luck so far.

More Details

Infernape EX

Set:
A2 | Space-Time Smackdown

I like this artwork a lot, mainly because the illustration seems to be based on the myth of Son Wukong; I'm unsure whether the Pokemon itself is based on this myth, but the pose and the art style are very unique.

More Details

Lucario

Set:
A2 | Space-Time Smackdown

I love the colors. This art style must have some kind of name to it; the way it almost looks "washed-out", in a positive sense, is very pleasing to the eye. Also, I like the diagonal lines; it gives the artwork a more abstract look in my opinion. Lastly, the way Lucario appears with his other Fighting-type comrades (possibly alluding to his Fighting Coach ability) is a nice little detail as well.

More Details

Manaphy

Set:
A2 | Space-Time Smackdown

I love this artwork. I think it's one of the cutest in the entire game. I love Manaphy's pose! Also, the artstyle gives out a sort of watercolor-y vibe. The other Water-type Pokemon around Manaphy is a nice touch as well! I use this card in my Water-type decks.

More Details

Pikachu EX

Set:
A2b | Shining Revelry

This artwork combines both "cool" and "beautiful" in a perfect sense. The almost monochrome-like color scheme, as well as the painting-like art style, makes this very unique in my opinion. Like I said, the attacking pose of Pikachu doing the Thunderbolt looks amazing! I've also wanted to get this card in-game to no luck.
